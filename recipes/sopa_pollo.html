Sopa de Pollo

Sopa de Pollo, a very delisious soup

This is a very delisious soup that is made out of vegetables and chicken, very popular and cheap too!

Now I will show you the ingredients that you need to prepare it

ingredients

  • chicken
  • Water
  • potato

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add the chicken
  3. Leave it boiling for around 30 minutes
  4. Share it with your family
