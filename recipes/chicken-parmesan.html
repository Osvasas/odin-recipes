Chicken Parmesan

Tasties chicken, for everybody

This chicken is one of my favorite ones, personally. A very delicate recipe, mixing different textures and flavours

Everybody likes this plate, though it cannot be found at any restaurants, if you find it, you are very lucky

Ingredients

  • skinless, boneless chicjen
  • 2 large eggs
  • black pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. place chicken breast between 2 sheets of plastic
  2. beat eggs
  3. mix bread crumbs
  4. transfer chicken to a baking dish
