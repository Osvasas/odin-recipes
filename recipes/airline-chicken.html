Airline Chicken

Very fancy and tasty chicken...

This chicken is very popular in the USA, and is one of the most fancy plates that you can find in the restaurants.

Though this chicken is one of the most delisious plates, it is not too expensive as people think

ingredients

  • Chicken
  • drizzle olive oil
  • ground black pepper
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. slice off the chicken
  2. Remove the tenders
  3. separate bone from meat
  4. pour stock into the pan
